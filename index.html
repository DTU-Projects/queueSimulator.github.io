    Kendall Notation (A/S/c):(K/N/D)

    A denotes the time between arrivals to the queue, S the service time distribution and c the number of service channels open at the node,
    K is the capacity of the queue, N is the size of the population of jobs to be served, and D is the queueing discipline.
